Learn to perform the Fusion Technique!

Step 1: Starting Position

Stand about 6 paces away from your partner with your feet apart and your arms extended in the opposite direction of your partner.

Step 2: Bring Feet Together

Bring your feet together with the heels touching while standing on your toes.

Step 3: Arm Movement

Bring your arms towards your partner in an arch over your head.

Step 4: Move Towards Your Partner

While you are moving your arms in you are also going to take three small steps towards the direction of your partner.
Make sure the arms and the steps are synchronized.

Step 5: Lift Knee Up

Both you and your partner will lift their outer knee up. Make sure the knee is pointed away from your partner.

Step 6: Arms Out/Knees In

Now you and your partner are going to swing your arms away from each other (like in the starting position). At the same time you will turn your knee toward your partner. This should be done simultaneously.

Step 7: Final Step

Extend your leg (with the knee up) out and away from each other while pointing your fingertips towards your partner. Touch each other's fingers to complete the fusion dance.

Step 8: Fusion Complete

Behold the new powerful warrior you have fused into. Admire yourself in the mirror. Make sure to give yourself a cool name that combines both you and your partner's names.
